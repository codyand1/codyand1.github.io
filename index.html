"The newest 5-star UGA Commit: Justin Fields"

Going into his senior season Justin Fields is the number 1 ranked player in his class in the nation. He currently plays for Harrison High School and is committed to play football for The University of Georgia. He is a dual threat QB meaning he can throw and run. He's 6'3 and 221 lbs and has a hell of an arm, he's also very fast at 221 lbs being able to run a 4.51 40-yard dash which is flying! He Originally committed to Penn State a little less then a year ago and decided to decommiitt about 6 months later. After decommiting he made his top 4 schools known: Auburn, Florida State, LSU and UGA. He made his hard commitment on October, 6th 2017 committing to play for Kirby Smart and The Dawgs!
